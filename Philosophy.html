Our Philosophy

The ultimate purpose of humanity is the observation of beauty.
Beauty is a sensation that occurs when we become aware of our own freedom.
This sublime awareness occurs through acts of creation or destruction.
Creation is the imposition of an internal order upon an existing external order.
Destruction is the reduction an existing order to a state of chaos.
The violent, chaotic destruction of an external order is a necessary and inextricable aspect of the creation of a new order.
There can be no logical distinction between acts of creation and destruction.
Art is the simulation of such sublime acts.

Therefore, our purpose is the relentless proliferation of both beauty and the chaos it requires to flourish.
